Welcome to Mountain Artisan Collective

Mountain Artisan Collective is located in the heart of Kathmandu, specializing in unique handmade handicrafts that capture the rich cultural heritage of Nepal. Our products, including intricate wood carvings, metal statues, Thangka paintings, and traditional scarves, are crafted by skilled artisans who have honed their crafts over generations.

We take pride in offering authentic, high-quality products that resonate with customers worldwide, from the USA and Canada to Europe and beyond. Explore our collection online and discover the timeless beauty of Nepali craftsmanship.
